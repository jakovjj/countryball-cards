Game Overview

1v1 tactical card game where you control nations in strategic combat. Deploy countryballs, manage resources, and eliminate your opponent's forces.

Players: 1v1 or 2v2
Time: 30-45 min
Age: 12+

Non-Attack Abilities

Some abilities provide other effects (healing, resource generation, etc.) but may still require dice rolls for success

Stun Mechanic

Stunned can't use abilities next turn, then automatically reactivate

Multiple Targets

When an ability affects multiple targets, roll separately for each one

Failed Attempts

Resources are still consumed even if the dice roll fails - risk vs reward!
