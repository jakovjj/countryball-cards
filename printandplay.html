How to Set Up

1

📥 Download & Print

Download the PDF and print all pages on standard A4 paper. Use cardstock for better durability.

2

✂️ Cut Out Components

Carefully cut out all cards, tokens, and game pieces along the marked lines. Consider using card sleeves.

3

🎲 Get Dice

You'll need a standard 20-sided die and four 6-sided dice. Or use our online dice roller tool.

4

🎮 Start Playing!

Follow the rules to set up your first game. Gather 2 or 4 players and enjoy strategic countryball action!
